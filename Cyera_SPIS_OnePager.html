Why this matters for coaching
When an RD opens SPIS before a 1:1, they can see exactly what the seller has entered versus what the system generated. If the pain hypothesis is thin, that's a coaching signal. If the competitive section is blank, that's another signal. The three-signal design makes the quality of the seller's thinking visible before the conversation starts — which means the RD can spend the coaching session on the actual gaps instead of asking the AE to explain their territory from scratch.
